  1. When the User uses the Website, data about him or her is automatically collected. This data includes IP address, domain name, browser type, and operating system type. This data can be collected by cookie files (cookies) and can also be saved in server logs.

Cookies remember the User’s preferences, which enables him or her to improve the quality of service, improve search results and the accuracy of the information displayed, as well as track the User’s preferences anonymously.

  3. The User consents to the Administrator’s storing or accessing cookies on the User’s device by means of the browser settings installed on the User's device.

  4. The User may opt out of cookies by selecting relevant settings in his or her web browser. In such an event, the use of the Website by the User may be hindered (e.g. the website may work more slowly).

  5. The User may delete existing cookies from his or her device by using relevant functions of his or her web browser or programs designed for this purpose, or by using relevant tools available under the operating system he or she is using.

  6. The use of the Website involves the transmission of queries to the server on which the Website is stored. Every such query is saved in the server logs. The logs include the User's IP address, the date and time of logging in to the server, as well as information about the web browser and operating system used.

The server logs are used to administrate the Website, and their content is not disclosed to anyone other than those authorised to administrate the server.
